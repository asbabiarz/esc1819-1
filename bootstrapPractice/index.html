Amiga Multitasking Operating System

Carl is famouse for his Amiga Operating system which allowed for multaple programs to take turns being shown to make them appear as if they were running at the same time. When the Commodore Amiga was released in 1985 it was the first multimedia personal computer on the market and featured a color graphical user interface, a two button mouse, a fast graphic subsystem, and support for animation and sound.
